    Course 1. The Threshold. The Scent Path Begins

    ABOUT

    You inhale — and enter.  It is not yet a deep journey, but no longer just a breath.  This is the threshold between the outer and the inner.  A space where the body starts to listen, and the soul begins to respond.   We will get to know wild plants of Israel — through scent.  Each plant is a voice of the land,  a touch to the subtle layers within,  where scent meets essence.   You will receive hydrosol essences — and begin a dialogue with the aroma.  First — simply listen.  Then — attune.  Then — hear.   This is the path of awakening perception.  A path back to yourself. 🌿 What Awaits You This is not just a course. This is a quiet return to scent as language. And to yourself — as one who remembers how to listen.  You will meet 12 wild plants of Israel — resilient, untamed, and patient. Each day — one plant, one scent, one inner space. These are not just aromas. They are invitations. To pause. To listen. To return to what is quiet inside.  Format: - Online - Self-paced journey  - Optional feedback and sharing  - One scent — one day  - Recommended length: 12 days  - No prerequisites — each level stands on its own (though we gently recommend taking them in sequence) You will receive: - Hand-distilled Hydrosol essences — 10ml bottles of each plant, sent by post  - Layered plant profiles — including botanical detail, cultural and spiritual context, psycho-emotional influence, and philosophical reflection  - Practices for each plant — from gentle introductions to deeper experiential exercises Course plants:  1. Fumana thymifolia  2. Notobasis syriaca  3. Inula viscosa (coastal form)  4. Vitex agnus-castus  5. Acanthus syriacus  6. Smilax aspera  7. Pinus halepensis  8. Stachys palaestina L.  9. Tamarix  10. Cistus creticus  11. Thymbra spicata  12. Satureja thymbra 📦 Note: Please provide the shipping address for sending the hydrosol essences when registering for the course.
